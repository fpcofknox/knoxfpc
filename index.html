ABOUT US

We call ourselves Pentecostal because we have experienced the same miraculous conversion that the 120 followers (which included the mother of Jesus) experienced in the second chapter of Acts.
According to the Bible (The Acts of the Apostles), the New Testament Church began on the Day of Pentecost, which was fifty days after the resurrection of Jesus Christ. When the crowd heard the noise, saw the commotion, and listened to the message, they asked the Apostles how to be saved.
Peter, standing up with the other eleven apostles, preached this message:

Repentance

We must repent of, or turn away from, our sins. We do this by asking Jesus to forgive us.
Luke 12:47; 2Peter 3:9, Acts 2:38

Baptism

We must be baptized (fully submerged in water) in the Name of Jesus Christ for the remission, or washing away, of our sins.
John 3:4; Acts 2:38, 8:16, 10:48, 19:5

Spirit Infilling

We are promised the gift of His Spirit with the evidence of speaking in other tongues.
John 7:37-39; Acts 2:4, 2:38, 8:17-18, 9:17, 10:44-47, 19:6

COME WORSHIP WITH US

Pastor Jerry Patrick
1-574-806-2055

It was in the year of 1992 when the work of the First Pentecostal Church here in Knox, Indiana began. The call began a few years earlier as God divinely called Jerry and Marlene Patrick to this community. it was in the month of July 1992 that Pastor Patrick and his wife Marlene (along with their three children April, Jeremiah, & Candice) obeyed that calling. They would leave their home and home church in Bourbon Indiana, with their Pastor's blessing, with a strong burden to spread the Gospel of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ and to preach the whole Bible to this community. They continue to this day 30+ years later to passionately labor for Jesus. He is so worthy!
